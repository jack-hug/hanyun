About Us

HANYUN MOLD was founded in 2010, is a factory specializing in the production of standard slide core unit, precision positioning, various types of sliders, non-standard slide core units etc. The company is located in Shenzhen and continues to develop with its superior geographical location. With more than 10 years of experience in slide core units, we have mastered every production step and know how to produce high-quality slides at low cost. In order to shorten the delivery time, we always keep a large of semi-inventory and finished products all over year. Looking forward to hearing from you soon.
